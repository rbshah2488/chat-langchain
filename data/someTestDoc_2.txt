SomeOtherVectorDB is an embedding database, one of the great ones.
SomeOtherVectorDB makes it easy to build LLM apps by making knowledge, facts, and skills pluggable for LLMs.
SomeOtherVectorDB gives you the tools to:
store embeddings and their metadata
embed documents and queries
search embeddings
SomeOtherVectorDB prioritizes:
simplicity and developer productivity
analysis on top of search
it also happens to be very quick
